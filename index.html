IVF Process

Step 01

Support & Guidance

Undergoing IVF treatment can be physically & emotionally challenging for couples. At Prime IVF, our experts provide valuable support and counseling before starting the procedure.

Step 02

Ovulation Induction & Monitoring

Fertility medications stimulate the ovaries to produce multiple eggs, which are monitored through ultrasound and blood tests.

Step 03

Egg Retrieval

After ovarian stimulation, mature eggs are retrieved using a minor surgical procedure under expert supervision.

Step 04

Sperm Collection

A sperm sample is collected from the male partner or a donor. In some cases, surgical extraction may be required.

Step 05

Embryo Transfer

A selected embryo is transferred into the uterus after fertilization under laboratory conditions.

Step 06

Post Transfer Care

Post embryo transfer, specialists may prescribe medication and routine care tips to enhance implantation chances.
